About Our School

A legacy of excellence in education since 1960

Shaping Futures Since 1960

Government High School Chak 237 JB has been a beacon of education in Tehsil Bhowana since 1960. Serving hundreds of students from Class 6 to Class 10, the school is recognized as one of the most distinguished government institutions in District Chiniot, Punjab.

Our dedicated faculty of 27 teachers works tirelessly to ensure that every student receives quality education, instilling values of discipline, hard work, and academic excellence that prepare them for a bright future.

Sports & Co-Curricular Activities

Building champions both on and off the field

At GHS Chak 237 JB, we believe in the holistic development of our students. Sports and co-curricular activities are an integral part of our curriculum. Our students actively participate in district and provincial level competitions, bringing pride to our school through Cricket, Football, Badminton, and Volleyball.

🏏

Punjab School Cricket Tournament

Our cricket team competes at district and provincial levels with distinction.
